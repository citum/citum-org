The citation lifecycle

A deterministic pipeline from structured markup to typeset output.

1

Data & Markup

Accept references in YAML or JSON, and citation markers directly in Djot or Markdown documents.

Citation markers (Djot)
[@kuhn1962]  # parenthetical
[+@kuhn1962, p. 10]  # integral
[-@kuhn1962, p. 10]  # suppress author
[@watson1953; @smith2010]  # multi-cite
Resolution trace
# Chapter 1
[+@smith2010] → Jane Doe (2010)
[@smith2010, p. 12] → (ibid., 12)
# Chapter 2 (scope reset)
[+@smith2010, p. 14] → Jane Doe (2010, 14)
2

Semantic Resolution

The engine resolves disambiguation, ibid tracking, and script-aware formatting rules.
