# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Researchers, academics, students, and librarians. They are often under pressure to meet strict formatting guidelines for publication or submission. They value accuracy over flashiness.

## Product Purpose
Citum provides a high-precision citation formatting engine that correctly handles complex academic edge cases (name disambiguation, ibid, source-type variations) that other tools often miss. It aims to reduce manual correction time.

## Brand Personality
Professional, precise, transparent, and authoritative. It should feel like a trusted tool for serious academic work.

## Anti-references
Generic "bento box" SaaS templates, over-animated "web3" landing pages, and opaque "it just works" marketing that hides failure points.

## Design Principles
- **Verifiable Accuracy**: Every formatting claim should be backed by visible evidence (like the compatibility report).
- **Focused Utility**: Prioritize information density and clarity over decorative whitespace.
- **Trust through Transparency**: Be honest about what is supported and what is still in development.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG 2.1 AA. Ensuring high contrast for long-form reading and clear focus states for keyboard users.
